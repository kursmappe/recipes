Ingredients:
 - 1-2 onions
 - 125g long-grain white rice
 - 250ml water
 - about 4 teaspoons curry
 - olive oil, salt, pepper

Directions:
  1. Cook rice as described on the packaging.
     Rule of thumb: Cook 125g rice in 250ml water for ~15 minutes
  2. Heat some oil in a pan and fry onions
  3. Reduce the heat and add the cooked rice to the onions
  4. Add curry to rice and fry it for a few minutes
  5. Flavor with salt and pepper as desired
